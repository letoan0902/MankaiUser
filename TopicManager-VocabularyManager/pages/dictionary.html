Kunyomi

みちび*く

to lead or guide - in the sense of MORAL guidance, reforming a juvenile delinquent, to lead by example, etc

Mnemonic

This street is dangerous, so follow and stick to your leader like glue

Jukugo

誘導

ゆうどう

Lead

誘 (invite / entice) + 導 (lead) = 誘導 (lead)

to lead or conduct : A teacher leads the class on the field trip. The policewoman conducts traffic. The nuance is, 誘導 means literally going together with the people to the place.

導入

どうにゅう

Introduce new concept

導 (lead) + 入 (put/go in) = 導入 (introduce new concept)

to lead or conduct : A teacher leads the class on the field trip. The policewoman conducts traffic. The nuance is, 導入 means introducing a new concept or system.
